I am a Ph.D. student working in the Cognitive Decision Making Lab at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign under the supervision of Dr. Jared Hotaling. I am interested in decision-making processes and decision theory.

I obtained my B.A. in both Cognitive Science and Philosophy from Carleton College. As an undergraduate at Carleton, I worked in the Language and Cognition Research Lab under the supervision of Dr. Mija Van Der Wege.

I am VoiD_Glitch on Reddit. I am a competitive Brawlhalla player (Esports Earnings), a former competitive MultiVersus player, and I used to play Call of Duty for Lethal Gaming. I was ranked in the top 125 most popular creators on Flipnote Hatena in the Americas region (Flipnote Archive).
