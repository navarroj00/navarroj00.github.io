About Me


I am a current student at Carleton College with the intention of double majoring in Computer Science and Psychology. Upon graduation from Carleton College, I plan to pursue a PhD.

I have finished writing an undergraduate philosophical text which attempts to interpret and evaluate contemporary societal issues and to respond to a selection of unsolved problems in philosophy. However, I have not begun to send drafts of my writing to any publishers as of yet; I am uncertain whether I should strive to issue it in the form of undergraduate student papers or a book.
