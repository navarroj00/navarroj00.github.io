Introduction

Void Relics became the principal means of acquiring Prime parts and Forma Blueprints when the Specters of the Rail Warframe update was pushed. Void Relics function in a manner exclusive to the random number generation algorithm used in Warframe. Their rarity weights are tabulated in the next section.
