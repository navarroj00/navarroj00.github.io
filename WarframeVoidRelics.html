Introduction

When Warframe Update: Specters of the Rail was pushed, Void Relics became the principal means of acquiring Prime and Forma Blueprints. Void Relics function in a manner exclusive to the random number generation algorithm used in Warframe. Their rarity weights are tabulated in the next section.
