Undergraduate Text

I have been in the process of writing an undergraduate text during my 2018 - 2019 Winter Break. It is a philosophical addression which attempts to interpret and evaluate contemporary societal issues and to respond to a selection of unsolved problems in philosophy.

I have not begun to send drafts of my writing to any publishers as of yet; I am uncertain whether I should strive to issue it in the form of undergraduate student papers or a book.
