Competitive Brawlhalla Player Profile

Power Rankings

A power ranking is a numerical representation of competitive strength, often directly comparable so that the game outcome between any two teams can be predicted. The Official Brawlhalla Power Rankings is a power ranking of Brawlhalla players (1v1, 2v2) in a particular region (either North America, Europe, South America, South East Asia, or Australia) based on the following factors: tournament placements, individual matchups (that is, who a players beats and loses to), tournament importance, and recency.
