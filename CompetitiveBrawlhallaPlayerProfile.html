Competitive Brawlhalla Player Profile

Power Rankings

A power ranking is a numerical representation of competitive strength, often directly comparable so that the game outcome between any two teams can be predicted. The Official Brawlhalla Power Rankings is a ranking of the top 250 Brawlhalla players (1v1, 2v2) in a particular region; these include North America, Europe, South East Asia, South America, and Australia. These power rankings are derived from the following factors: tournament placements, individual matchups (that is, who a players beats and loses to), tournament importance, and recency.
