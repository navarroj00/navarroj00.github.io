Informal Research

  • Random Number Generation in Warframe

  • Random number generation is the generation of a sequence of numbers or symbols that cannot be reasonably predicted better than by a random chance. In this paper I disclose the process of random number generation in the video game Warframe and clarify how it operates. Understanding random number generation is pivotal to the way individuals make decisions based on chance; it is my firm belief that those who demonstrate a consistency in logical reasoning will be more enticed to make informed decisions, and understanding the methodology to the system that is randomness and how it has been manipulated within the context of this video game is important to those who decide to participate in it. I have obtained the information presented in the paper through executable analysis, predictive analysis, and game testing with a dedicated group of theory testers. "Random Number Generation in Warframe" Paper
