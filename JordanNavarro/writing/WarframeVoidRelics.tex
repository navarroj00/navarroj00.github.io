\section{Introduction}

Upon the launch of Warframe Update: Specters of the Rail, a new way to acquire Prime items, Void Relics, was introduced. Void Relics function in a manner exclusive to the random number generation algorithm used in Warframe. Their rarity weights are tabulated in the next section.
